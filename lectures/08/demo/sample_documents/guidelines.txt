Clinical Practice Guidelines Summary
=====================================

Hypertension Management (JNC8)
- Stage 1: SBP 130-139 or DBP 80-89 mmHg
- Stage 2: SBP ≥140 or DBP ≥90 mmHg
- First-line: thiazides, ACE inhibitors, ARBs, CCBs
- Lifestyle: DASH diet, sodium <2300mg/day, exercise 150 min/week

Type 2 Diabetes (ADA Standards of Care)
- Diagnosis: FPG ≥126, HbA1c ≥6.5%, or 2h glucose ≥200
- First-line: metformin
- Target HbA1c <7% for most adults
- With CVD: prefer SGLT2i or GLP-1 RA with proven CV benefit

Acute Chest Pain (AHA Guidelines)
- HEART score: History, ECG, Age, Risk factors, Troponin
- Low risk (0-3): discharge with follow-up
- Intermediate (4-6): admit for observation
- High risk (7-10): early invasive strategy
- STEMI: ST elevation ≥1mm in 2 contiguous leads, door-to-balloon <90 min

Drug Interactions
- Metformin + iodinated contrast: hold 48h before/after (lactic acidosis risk)
- Warfarin + NSAIDs/aspirin: increased bleeding risk
- ACE inhibitors + K-sparing diuretics: hyperkalemia risk
